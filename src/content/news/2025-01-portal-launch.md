The Center for Applied Computing (NCA) at the Federal University of Maranhão (UFMA) launches its new institutional portal, designed to present in an organized and accessible way the group's full scientific output, ongoing projects, and researcher team.

The portal consolidates information previously scattered across multiple channels, offering a centralized experience for industrial partners, academic collaborators, students interested in joining the group, and the scientific community in general.
