The SmartView suite consolidates four interrelated P&D projects delivered for CHESF in partnership with UFCG under ANEEL funding.

**SmartView GIS Base** presents CHESF's full transmission network — hydroelectric plants, thermal plants, substations, 230 kV / 500 kV lines — on an interactive web map, with real-time data feeds from SCADA/EMS, alarm dashboards, power quality indicators (power quality metrics), and oscillography records accessible by clicking any network element.

**SmartView Lightning and Wildfires** integrates atmospheric lightning discharge data and wildfire occurrence records, correlating environmental events with electrical grid incidents on the timeline view. Operators can identify whether a line fault coincides with a nearby wildfire or lightning strike with a single click.

**Environmental GIS — Reservoir Borders** provides spatial supervision of reservoir border areas and surrounding land use, supporting CHESF's environmental compliance obligations under ANEEL regulations. The system was extended with a full environmental licensing module featuring geospatial workflows for permit submission, spatial queries, and reporting.
