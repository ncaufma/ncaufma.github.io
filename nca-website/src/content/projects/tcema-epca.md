The TCE-MA engagement produced two complementary systems modernizing the public accountability process for the state of Maranhão.

**E-PCA (Electronic Annual Accounts Submission System)** allows thousands of jurisdictioned entities — municipalities, state agencies, public companies — to submit their annual accounts digitally. Entities select their reporting year and entity type, generate required modules, attach supporting documents, and submit through a structured online workflow. Auditors receive structured digital data instead of paper dossiers, dramatically accelerating the preliminary review phase.

**META (Auditor Workbench)** provides a data science-driven analytics environment for auditors. The flagship visualization cross-plots total creditors in payroll sheets against employees in salary accumulation situations across multiple administrative entities and risk bands (low, medium-low, medium-high, high). Anomalous entities — those with disproportionately high accumulation rates relative to peer organizations — are instantly visible, directing audit resources toward the highest-risk cases.
